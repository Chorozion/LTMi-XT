The Cold Storage line is the high-margin part of the business. We currently operate three trucks across the metro area. Each truck is staffed with one driver and one technician on long routes. Last quarter we lost two shipments to coolant mismatch which cost us roughly $4,800 in claims. The standard workflow is: customer calls in, dispatch logs the pickup, driver collects, technician verifies temperature on arrival, we deliver, the customer signs for it. For dry ice loads we always use the VIP panel inserts. They cost more up front but the failure rate is essentially zero. Gel pack loads are cheaper but only safe for transit times under 18 hours. Anything longer should be dry ice. The Sophia Key 3M tool can model a lane and tell us in advance which configuration will hold.
